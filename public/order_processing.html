How Do I Order?
  • If you would like to place an order, please fill in the enquiry form on the relevant product page and we will be back in touch very shortly via text/whatsapp or email.
  • If you are local to Harpenden, we offer free next-day delivery.
  • For postal orders there is a delivery charge of £3.95, or free for orders over £75.
  • Payment can be made by wire transfer once we’ve received your order.
